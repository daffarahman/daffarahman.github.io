About the Game
Off The Tracks is an arcade racing game about controlling your car to stay on the track. It's a little bit hard but it's gonna be fun once you get used to it. And this game is endless so you can play as long as you can. This is the first mobile game I've ever made and published, so if you found any problems or bugs, feel free to put it on the review or email me.

Features
  • Endless gameplay, play as long as you can, the longer the better
  • One car with more cars coming in the future updates
  • Challenging track
  • Music
